About Kisan AI.

A free AI assistant built for the field, not the office.

Kisan AI is a free assistant that helps farmers diagnose crop diseases from a photo, check live weather, track mandi prices, and get answers to everyday farming questions — all in the farmer's own language.

No subscription. No hidden cost. No app store fee. Just open it in a browser or add it to your home screen, and it works like any other app.

Our approach.

How we approach Kisan AI.

Language first.

Every answer is built to be understood in the farmer's own language first, not translated as an afterthought.

Zero cost, always.

Built entirely on free tools, so cost is never the reason a farmer goes without good advice.

Answers, not jargon.

Every response is short, direct and actionable — what to do, how much, and when.

The reality.

The reality of Indian agriculture.

Delayed diagnosis.

A sick crop is often noticed too late, after the disease has already spread through the field.

Kisan AI diagnoses from a single photo, in seconds.

No local-language advice.

Most expert agricultural advice exists only in English, out of reach for most farmers.

Kisan AI answers in the farmer's own language, every time.

Guessing the market.

Without live mandi data, produce often gets sold at the wrong price, in the wrong market.

Kisan AI shows today's prices before the truck is loaded.

Cost of expert advice.

A private agronomist visit can cost more than a farmer can spare in a tight season.

Kisan AI is completely free, every season.
